Diablo 42

A 42 rush

I built this role-playing hack-and-slash computer video game in two days using unity with C#. The player takes the role of Maya, a sorceress against zombies. Maya can loot items (randomly generated equipment divided into several quality levels), cast spells. I made the game as close to diablo II as possible, the player has a set of skills and attributes to choose from so they can scale Maya the way they wish : could be a healer or a damage dealer !
